Northwest side

A village in Lancashire has been named as one of the top 12 coolest postcodes to live in.

Aughton (L39), in Northwest Lancashire near Ormskirk, has made the list and is a place to go for serious foodies, according to The Times. After two years of being cramped in city centres with little outdoor space, many people, particularly Millenials, are swapping city life for the countryside and coastal areas. Also featured on the list was Ancoats in Manchester (M4), Hockley in Nottingham (NG1) and seaside town Mumbles in Swansea (SA3).
